\begin{abstract}
We propose ALMS (Algorithm for Locating Maximally Similar entities), a novel geodesic attention layer that performs topological diffusion on Riemannian similarity manifolds. Unlike traditional attention, ALMS uses spectral geodesic distances and harmonic potentials to enhance feature representations under differential privacy guarantees. Experiments show robustness across batch sizes and scalable performance on high-dimensional data.
\end{abstract}
